The toolkit

Six focused tools for project work

Each tool covers a practical area of non-profit operations. They work together or independently depending on your workflow.

📊
DESy Project accounting

Project accounting and financial coordination. Track project accounts, budget lines, expenses and financial coordination across activities.

📈
PREPPY Budget forecasting

Dynamic budget forecasting and expenditure planning. Anticipate future spending, compare forecasts with budgets and identify risks.

📉
Geko Cost allocation

Sustainability analysis and cost allocation. Support allocation of shared costs across projects and understand financial sustainability.

👥
RICO HR management

Human resources, contracts and attendance. Keep staff information, contractual details and records organized.

📦
AIDA Asset tracking

Asset Inventory & Depreciation Application. Track equipment, assets and inventory for project accountability and internal control.

📋
PAS Procurement monitoring

Procurement monitoring and procedural compliance. Keep procurement steps, documentation and procedural requirements visible and traceable.
